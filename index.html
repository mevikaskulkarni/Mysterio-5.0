About Mysterio 5.0

Mysterio 5.0 is a premier technical symposium organized by the Department of Electronics and Communication, JNNCE, Shivamogga. Click to know more.

Mysterio 5.0 brings together innovators, tech enthusiasts, and problem solvers to participate in challenging and creative events.

About the College

JNNCE was established in 1980. Click to know more.

JNNCE is affiliated with VTU, Belagavi, and recognized by AICTE. The campus is spread across 39.4 acres with state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty.
